Ecosystem

Collection

Stations are placed at lightning-frequent locations. A single bolt can contain up to one billion volts
of electricity. A typical station can withstand multiple strikes simultaneously and reduce volts and
ampheres to safe levels in seconds.

Storage

Power warehouses are vast facilities storing energy collected from nearby stations. Thanks to
superconductivity, these massive batteries can hold electricity with minimal loss.

Distribution

Power warehouses are actually battery storages, which means they can distribute power via vehicles in
addition to nation grids. Drained batteries will then be returned to power warehouses for recharging.
